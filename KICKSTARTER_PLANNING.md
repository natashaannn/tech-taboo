## Risk Mitigation

### Manufacturing Risks

**Risk:** Production delays
- **Mitigation:** Build 4-week buffer into timeline, choose reliable manufacturer with references

**Risk:** Quality issues
- **Mitigation:** Order pre-production samples, conduct quality inspections, have backup manufacturer

**Risk:** Cost overruns
- **Mitigation:** 20% contingency budget, locked-in quotes from manufacturers

### Fulfillment Risks

**Risk:** Shipping delays or damage
- **Mitigation:** Insurance on shipments, partner with experienced fulfillment center, pack protectively

**Risk:** Customs issues for international backers
- **Mitigation:** Clear communication about potential customs fees, consider regional warehouses if stretch goal hit

### Campaign Risks

**Risk:** Not reaching funding goal
- **Mitigation:** Strong pre-launch list building, realistic goal setting, engaged community

**Risk:** Overwhelming success beyond capacity
- **Mitigation:** Clear communication about timelines, scale manufacturing gradually, hire help if needed
