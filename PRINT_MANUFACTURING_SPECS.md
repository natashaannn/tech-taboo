## Universal Manufacturing Requirements

### Print Quality Standards

- **Color Accuracy:** CMYK process, color-matched to approved proofs
- **Registration:** Perfect alignment on both sides
- **Bleed:** 3mm bleed on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 5mm from edge for critical text/graphics
- **Finish Quality:** No scratches, scuffs, or imperfections

### Card Stock Details: Germany 310 Black Core

- **Weight:** 310gsm
- **Core:** Black core layer (visible on card edges)
- **Benefits:**
  - Premium feel and durability
  - Enhanced opacity (no show-through)
  - Professional appearance with black edge reveal
  - Excellent snap and shuffle quality

### Quality Control

- **Inspection:** 100% visual inspection for defects
- **Cutting Tolerance:** ±0.5mm
- **Corner Radius:** Standard poker radius (3mm)
- **Color Consistency:** Delta E < 2.0 across entire print run

### Companion Book Specifications (All Versions)

- **Paper Stock:** 120gsm uncoated or 150gsm coated
- **Printing:** Full color, both sides
- **Binding:** Saddle stitch (≤24 pages) or perfect bound (>24 pages)
- **Size:** Custom sized to fit respective packaging
- **Content Structure:**
  - Alphabetical index of terms
  - Clear, concise definitions
  - Example usage or context where applicable
  - Page numbers and easy navigation

## Cost Optimization Notes

1. **Linen Finish:** Optional - evaluate cost vs. perceived value
2. **Packaging:** Rigid boxes add significant cost but enhance perceived value
3. **Book Binding:** Saddle stitch is cheaper than perfect binding for lower page counts
4. **Print Runs:** Larger quantities significantly reduce per-unit cost
5. **Stretch Goal:** Presenter version should be priced to cover higher production costs

1. **Initial Quote Request:** Send specifications to 3-5 manufacturers
2. **Sample Request:** Order physical samples of card stock and finishes

4. **Production Approval:** Final sign-off on samples before mass production
